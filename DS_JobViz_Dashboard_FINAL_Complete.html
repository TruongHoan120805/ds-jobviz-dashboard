Domain Tasks & Design Rationale

Đồ án Biểu diễn dữ liệu • Tiết Gia Hồng • HCMUS

DATA PROFILING & ABSTRACTION (20%)

Attribute Types: Quantitative (avg_salary, company_age, rating), Nominal (job_simp, state, seniority, industry), Binary (skills flags).

Cleaning steps: Parsed salary ranges → min/max/avg; extracted state codes; binned company size; computed company_age (2021 - Founded); keyword-based skill extraction with regex word boundaries from Job Description; handled -1/missing as "Unknown" or NaN.

DOMAIN TASKS (20%)
Task 1: Salary Landscape Exploration
Who: Job seekers, career switchers, students | Why: Understand compensation drivers to negotiate and target opportunities | What: Salary distribution by location/seniority/company size; correlation with company rating.
Task 2: Skills Demand & Market Alignment
Who: Learners, educators, talent teams | Why: Identify high-value skills and skill combinations for career growth | What: Prevalence ranking of technical skills; how skills relate to higher salary / senior roles via interactive filtering.
IDIOM DESIGN (20%)
Bar Chart (States) — Length channel for accurate comparison of counts. Ordered descending for quick ranking.
Histogram + Brush — Density + interactive range selection. Classic for distribution tasks.
Scatter Plot — Position (x=rating, y=salary) for correlation judgment. Color (seniority) + size encoding adds dimensions without clutter. (Đã thêm Zoom & Pan)
Horizontal Bars (Skills) — Length for % demand. Tooltip shows exact count + salary lift when filtered.
TỰ ĐÁNH GIÁ (Perceptual & Technical Evaluation)
Điểm mạnh:
  • Cross-filtering mạnh, phản hồi nhanh với 672 records
  • 4 idioms đa dạng, kênh thị giác phù hợp (length, position, color)
  • Self-contained → dễ nộp và chạy ở mọi máy
  • Tooltip + Brush + Click-to-filter đầy đủ yêu cầu tương tác
  • Giao diện hiện đại, dễ sử dụng, responsive
Điểm cần cải thiện:
  • Chưa có Zooming mặc định trên tất cả biểu đồ (chỉ có ở Scatter)
  • Bảng dữ liệu chỉ hiển thị 50 dòng đầu (có thể cải thiện pagination)
  • Chưa có dark mode (có thể thêm nếu cần)
Technical notes: Pure D3.js v7 + Tailwind. Cross-filtering via reactive JS state. All idioms respect Abstraction (data types) and use appropriate visual channels. Perceptual: high contrast, clear labels, responsive tooltips. Performance optimized for 672 records. Zoom & Pan đã được thêm vào Scatter Plot.
